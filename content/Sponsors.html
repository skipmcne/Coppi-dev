Title Sponsor:  Vapiano USA

Somewhere between the nicest of fast casual and hippest of casual dining restaurants is VAPIANO. Defining the future of fresh casual - a new and refreshing niche in the restaurant industry - Vapiano is an innovative European concept serving made-to-order hand tossed pizzas, fresh, house-made pasta and hand tossed gourmet salads. An established concept with more than 120 worldwide locations in 26 countires and another many more in development, Vapiano puts a new spin on the dining experience with its urban upscale Italian decor and its modern customer service. Vapiano is a fast casual restaurant with a twist - customers use a "chip card" to personally order their food or drinks from the bar or from the individual fresh pizza, pasta or salad stations. With this new innovative technology, Vapiano is going to change the way restaurants do business!

Freshbikes

As one of the premier bike shops in Northern Virginia and Montgomery County Maryland, Freshbikes (formerly Conte’s) is much more than a bike shop, and is truly a boutique cycling destination. Freshbikes' selection, service and expertise is unrivaled among even the best bike shops. With many of today’s top brands on display and in stock, Freshbikes is the cycling store for any performance-minded cyclist, be it road, triathlon, cyclocross, mountain bike, or performance hybrid bicycles.  They are the largest Cannondale, Cervelo, Pinarello & Willier dealer on the East Coast and also carry bikes from Specialized and Ridley.  Each year they are recognized by Bicycle Retailer as one of the top retailers in the nation.  

Supporting Squadra Coppi:  Freshbikes has supported our team and our races with generous donations and discounts that make it possible for our members to compete throughout the region as well as to promote our events. 

Visit Freshbikes website

Core Wellness & Physical Therapy

We could not stay in good shape and on the road without the help of our sponsors at Core Wellness and Physical Therapy. They are different from traditional physical therapy clinics. Their approach to health, wellness, prevention, and treatment occurs in one-on-one treatment sessions, with a highly experienced physical therapist. From your initial evaluation until the day you are discharged, you will receive personalized care using integrated techniques including manual therapy, exercise prescription, comprehensive education about your condition, and lifestyle modification. To serve you best, they do not allow your insurance company to dictate the care you will receive.

Visit Core Wellness & Physical Therapy website

Bikeman.com

Nestled on the coast of Maine is a quiet bike shop called Bath Cycle and Ski, but it has a dirty secret.  Bath Cycle and Ski is also known online as Bikeman.  Originally started in the 90's as a mail order catalog available out of the back of rags like Mountain Bike Action, the Bikeman has perfected the art of delivering all sorts of unique parts at seriously competitive prices.  Whether you need a 7 speed shifter for your mountain bike or the latest carbon titanium crankset from Italy, the Bikeman can get to for you and you'll be happy with the price. Check em out online or give them a call, they know bikes and who knows what they have in the basement waiting for the perfect buyer.
