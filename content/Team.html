About Squadra Coppi

Squadra Coppi/Vapiano USA is an Arlington, VA based bicycle racing team. Famous for our esprit de corps, Coppi riders are well-known for supporting their own on and off the bike. Join us on one of our team rides and see if Squadra Coppi is a fit for you--it's how every team member got their start with us.
